Color Grid

Hello, and welcome to Color Grid, an online application where you can create and share pixel art creations!

After signing in, you can head on over to the create page and get to work on a pixel art creation!

Select from the variety of colors and click the grid squares to fill them in. With our 10x10 grid and 10 differnet colors, the possibilites are nearly limitless!

After completing your masterpiece and giving it a title, you can click the finish button to upload your work to the gallery!

Happy pixeling!
